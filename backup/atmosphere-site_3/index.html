At Atmosphere, we are contextual designers rooted in the Indian subcontinent. Our practice spans architecture, interiors, and hospitality — each project approached as a singular conversation between craft, climate, and culture.

From the warm materiality of raw concrete and timber to the refined calm of sculpted interiors, we draw from living craft traditions, reinterpreted through a rigorous contemporary lens.
