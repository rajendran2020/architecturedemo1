"The most enduring architecture is that which listens to its land before it speaks."

At Mehta & Varma, we are rooted contextualists. Every project begins with an act of careful listening — to the site, its climate, its cultural memory, and the people who will inhabit it.

We draw from Rajasthani vernacular wisdom, Mughal spatial logic, and the artisanship of India's living craft traditions — reinterpreted through a rigorous contemporary lens.
