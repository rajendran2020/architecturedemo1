Architecture is not decoration.
It is the structure of life itself.

Studio Iyer was founded in 2005 on a single conviction: that buildings must earn their place in the world. Not through spectacle, but through rigorous honesty — in material, in programme, in structure.

We are a Bengaluru-based practice working across scales, from intimate residences to urban infrastructure. We believe that the most radical act in contemporary architecture is restraint — the discipline to do less, better.
